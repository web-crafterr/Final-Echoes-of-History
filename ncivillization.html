Ancient Egypt

Ancient Egypt thrived along the Nile for thousands of years, creating some of the world’s most iconic monuments. Its people developed early writing, religion, art, and advanced knowledge in science and mathematics. The legacy of its pharaohs, temples, and pyramids still shapes our understanding of ancient history.
