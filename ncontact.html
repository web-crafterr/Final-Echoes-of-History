About Project

Echoes of History is a visually engaging and beginner-friendly web project designed to present ancient civilizations in a clear, modern, and immersive way. The website combines short, easy-to-read content with aesthetic imagery, structured layouts, and smooth visual elements to make historical learning more enjoyable. Each section is thoughtfully arranged to avoid clutter and maintain a clean user experience. Through curated visuals, concise explanations, and an elegant design style, the project offers a refined introduction to major civilizations and their cultural impact—making history accessible, appealing, and meaningful for every visitor.
